About me

This section is about my academic and professional life.

  • 2012

    My Beginnings

    When I got out of school, I studied Law for a year and a half, had good grades, but didn't like it. I then studied Physics for a year and a half, not getting good grades, as I was very lazy.

  • 2015-2018

    I left

    In 2015, I left for Sweden. Then, after encountering personal problems, I had to go to Paris, my second home and stay there. That's where I started my journey into programming. I studied Copmuter Science for almsot 3 years.

  • 2018

    My First Job

    In 2018, I got my first job as an IT helpdesk assistant. Then I got my first permanent job, which I kept for one year. Then I finally got the job I have now, which is amazing, with amazing people. I am actually a COBOL Software Engineer and work as a contractor for a company at BNP Paribas.

  • 2021

    My Dream Job
